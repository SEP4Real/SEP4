| Phrase        | Definition                                                                                                         |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| The box       | Our physical device that measures data and displays information and the user can physically interact with.         |
| Measurement   | A singular number measured by a sensor at one point in time.                                                       |
| Sensor        | Device that measures environmental input.                                                                          |
| Status        | All the physical and measurable qualities of the current environment at a point in time.                            |
| Conditions    | Interpretations of the status. Can be any kind of quality and its interpretation.                                  |
| Rating        | A subjective user input classifying the perceived quality of a study session or current status of the environment. |
| Study Session | A period in time during which Students try to learn.                                                               |

# Glossary

# Actors

## Student

Someone who tries to study. They use the box to understand and improve conditions in their current study environment.

## Teacher

Someone who tries to teach others. They use the box to understand and improve conditions in their current teaching environment.
